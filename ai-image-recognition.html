AI Learning: Image Recognition

Learn how AI can recognize images by training it yourself!

Train Your Own AI!

In this activity, you'll teach an AI to recognize different types of images. Follow these steps:

  1. Select an image from the dropdown
  2. Add it to a category (animals, vehicles, or food)
  3. Add at least 2 images to each category
  4. Click "Train AI" to teach the AI
  5. Select a new image and click "Test AI" to see what it learned!
